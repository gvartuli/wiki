Overview
Centennial, Colorado is where Giovanna Vartuli has lived for most of her life. She moved there when she was about two years old and remained in the same house until her high school graduation. After graduation, she moved up to Fort Collins to go to school at Colorado State University.

While Giovanna technically lives in Fort Collins, she frequently drives the hour and a half drive it takes to get to Centennial, mostly to visit her family, largely on special occasions. Giovanna has made the trip down to Centennial for many reasons, be it for a school break or for a birthday or to go to the museum or the theatre in Denver (which is very close in location to Centennial).
