Overview of Life
Julia Vartuli was born in Colorado in 2001, and became the third and youngest child of Diane and Anthony. She, like her siblings, went to Franklin Elementary School, Powell Middle School, and eventually Arapahoe High School.

Julia, much like her brother, Michael, went on to play tennis all the way into high school, where she landed a Doubles Varsity spot on the women’s tennis team her Freshman year. Her team has gone to state multiple times, and she became Captain of her team her Senior year. Besides playing tennis, Julia also was a part of the club DECA, where she went to national’s her Junior year for an amazing video she created. She plans to go to national’s again during her Senior year.

Julia has had a love of filmmaking since she was in elementary school, when she would make a bunch of videos with her friends and family, including Giovanna, and has continued that love for it so much that she plans to study when she enters college. She just received an acceptance letter from Biola University, where her brother went, and she plans to go, as the school has an incredible and prestigious film program.
